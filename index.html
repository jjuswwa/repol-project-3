About Us

your legal partner & geodetic engineer

Repol Law Office and Land Surveying Services is led by Atty. Mary Jane Repol, a skilled geodetic engineer and bar passer. Her journey began with a deep-rooted passion for land surveying, nurtured by her father, Engr. Daniel Yonzon, a civil engineer and real estate broker who founded Yonzon Realty and Surveying.

Together, they ventured into Landlimits, a land surveying company where Atty. Mary Jane managed operations. Her recent admission to the bar prompted the creation of Repol Law Office, combining legal knowledge with geodetic expertise.

At Repol Law Office, we use our proficiency in legal and geodetic solutions to safeguard your land rights.
